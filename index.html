About Me

I’m a Product Manager that loves creating impactful products that scale. I’ve launched and iterated user-facing experiences in the B2B SaaS space – leading features across data privacy, email communication, onboarding and more.

Like many PMs, my path to it was fairly unconventional. A life of commitment to the idea of becoming a doctor sent me to medical school at 20. But experiences with economics and connected systems showed me the promise of data to change the world at scale, inspiring me to return to UCLA to complete a degree in Economics

After graduating, I spent the first half of my career leading strategy and execution in marketing. My approaches centered on building systems to efficiently connect people to solutions at scale. Seeing a frequent mismatch between peoples’ motivations and products created for them led me to seek an impact in product development. I now love leading teams to have a measurable impact and can’t see myself anywhere else.

I specialize in: strategic analysis (market, competitive, product, customer/user), opportunity discovery, value-based prioritization, personas, user stories, information architecture, wireframing, and MVP-driven hypothesis testing. I quantitatively model systems whenever I can to help inform where my team’s energy can have a disproportionate impact.
